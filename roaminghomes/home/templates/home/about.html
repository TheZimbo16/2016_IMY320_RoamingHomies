ABOUT US

OUR STORY

Roaming Homes is a Proudly South African Non-Profit Organisation. We offer shelter, food and clothes to those in need through our collection of several repurposed city buses. These are thoroughly equipped for their purpose, providing bunk beds and ablution facilities and offering South Africans in need a safe place to sleep and wash up, as well as receive a meal.

Roaming homes relies on the donations and help of the community. As a NPO we are always in need of volunteers and donations in the form of clothes, tinned foods, bedding, toiletries and monetary donations.
