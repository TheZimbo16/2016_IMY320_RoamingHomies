About

Roaming Homes

Roaming Homes is a Proudly South African Non-Profit Organisation aimed at offering shelter, food and clothes to those in need through a collection of repurposed city buses. There are several buses, all equipped with bunk beds and ablution facilities, that travel around Johannesburg, offering those in need a safe place to sleep and wash up, as well as receive a meal.

Roaming homes relies on the donations and help of the community. As a NPO we are always in need of volunteers and donations in the form of clothes, tinned foods, bedding, toiletries and monetary donations.
